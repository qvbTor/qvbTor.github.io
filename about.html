About Me

My name is Victor B. Ortega. I am 21 years old, born on June 7, 2002, and I reside in San Mateo, Rizal. I am currently studying Computer Engineering at the Technological Institute of the Philippines.

Currently in my third year of college, I am pursuing the System Administration track to gain a deeper understanding of the intricacies involved in managing computer systems. This program allows me to delve into various aspects of system administration, such as configuring and maintaining servers, managing user accounts, and ensuring the overall efficiency and security of computer networks. Through this specialized track, I aim to develop the skills necessary for effective system administration, preparing myself for the dynamic challenges in the field of information technology.
